### Resume Customizer

**Generate tailored resumes with your resume bank and get interview-ready **

[![Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/version-1.0.0-blue)](./resume-customizer/) [![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-stable-green)](./resume-customizer/)

#### Overview
Generate customized resumes in 5 minutes with intelligent relevance scoring, company research, and brutally honest recruiter-level feedback based on the job you're applying for. Share your success stories.

#### What It Does
- Analyzes job descriptions and researches companies
- Ranks your experiences by relevance (0-10 scale)
- Generates customized resume + recruiter assessment + interview prep
- Discovers undocumented experiences through conversation
- Provides honest gap analysis with mitigation strategies
- Creates self-improving library that gets better over time

#### Key Features
- **Intelligent Relevance Scoring** - Ranks every experience 0-10 for the target role
- **Honest Gap Analysis** - Identifies weaknesses and suggests strategies
- **Experience Discovery** - Interactive session to surface undocumented work
- **Company Research** - Automatic analysis of mission, culture, competitors
- **Self-Improving** - Each resume enriches your library for future use
- **Truth-Preserving** - Never fabricates, but intelligently reframes

#### Requirements
- Claude Pro or Team account (Extended Thinking required)
- Resume databank (master document with ALL your experiences, 10-20 pages)
- Job description (full posting text)

#### What You Get
1. Customized resume (.docx)
2. Recruiter assessment (fit score 0-100)
3. Interview preparation guide
4. Experience relevance rankings
5. Company research summary
6. Generation report with transparency

#### Perfect For
- Tailoring resumes for specific job applications
- Career transitions (different industry/role)
- Internal mobility (same company, different team)
- Multiple concurrent applications
- Recent graduates with limited experience
- Executive/C-level positions
- Career gaps (startups, sabbaticals)
- International applications

#### Real Results
- **Fit Score:** 92/100 (Exceptional Match) for OpenAI ChatGPT Growth PM role
- **Processing Time:** 8 minutes
- **JD Coverage:** 90%+
- **Top Experience:** AI platform scaling 24x identified as 9.8/10 relevance
- **Outcome:** Perfect positioning as "Growth PM Who Understands AI Products"

#### Quick Start
```
1. Upload skill to Claude Pro/Team
2. Prepare resume databank (master doc with all experiences)
3. Paste job description
4. Get complete package in 8-15 minutes
```
